Our History

TechCon was first established in 1996 in New Orleans, Louisiana, with the vision of providing a platform for technological advancement and expert collaboration in the tech sector. Over the decades, TechCon has grown to attract hundreds of innovators, industry leaders, and engineers from around the world.

Known for its dynamic discussions and practical sessions, the conference continually adapts to the evolving landscape of technological innovation and asset management. Each year, it is hosted in a major US city to accommodate its expanding audience, featuring facility tours and showcases from utility industry leaders.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, excellence, and education within the global technology community. By connecting industry experts, aspiring technologists, and thought leaders, TechCon empowers professionals to share forward-thinking solutions, explore emerging trends, and build lasting relationships.

The conference is dedicated to supporting growth, inclusion, and the exchange of knowledge to shape the future of technology.
